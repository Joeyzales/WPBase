My name is Vladimir Joseph Gonzales. I graduated from DeVry University in 2005 with a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Information Systems. Immediately after graduation, I began my career as a Software Developer, gaining experience across various industries. Over the past 20+ years, I have specialized in the Microsoft .NET Framework, working with languages such as C#, VB.NET, JavaScript, jQuery, and managing Microsoft SQL databases.

Around 2010, I developed a curiosity and passion for the film arts. A friend of mine was seeking Production Assistants, and I volunteered to help. This experience allowed me to work closely with industry professionals, enabling me to rapidly advance through different roles—starting as a PA, then moving to Gaffer, Sound Technician, and eventually Camera Assistant. It was during this journey that I fell in love with cinematography.

Balancing my full-time career as a Software Developer, I pursued my passion on the side—purchasing my own equipment and founding my company, Weekend Pictures. Over time, I built a network of talented collaborators, worked on various film sets, and even earned my own IMDB profile. This dual path has allowed me to combine technical expertise with creative expression, enriching my journey in both fields.
